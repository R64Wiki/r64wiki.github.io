Characters From Robot 64, in order of Appearance.

Lets start with...

Turtle Tops

Rabboy, a NPC, theyre local residents of the turtle.

theres 2 variants of rabboy, a speedboy, which will race you, and a sitting rabboy.

Manwog, Enemy, they damage you in collision.

Knoddy's Resort

Resident, a NPC, theyre local residents of the resort.

theres 1 variant of the residents, coolbros, which will challenge you to get points with the skateboard.

Guards, Enemy, they damage you in collision and will chase you down if youre in range.

Knody, Enemy, gets on an ufo and shoots surfboards from the residents, becomes a pickable after boss battle.

The Hecklands

Heckboy, a NPC, They reside in the hecklands.

Leader of the hecklands, is an enemy and has a boss battle.

Resident of the hecklands, you bully him for an icecream.
